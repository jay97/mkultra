About MKULTRA:

MKULTRA, once the brainchild of songwriter producer Ian Knapp, has now evolved into a collective effort, coming full circle from the project's earliest Trap Metal/SoundCloud Rap roots. Joining together with Danny Eberle, Alex Wacksman, Nate Holloway and Shalit Maitrechit, MKULTRA pulls from the sounds of hip hop, nu metal, death metal, hardcore, and 2000s pop.

Their new EP, "The Shape of Everything And Nothing To Come," is the blueprint for a sound that pays homage to the past while looking towards the future. Expect the unexpected.
